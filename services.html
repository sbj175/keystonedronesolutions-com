Off-the-shelf drone services don't always fit. We design and build end-to-end automated workflows tailored to your specific operational needs — combining drone data capture with custom software to solve problems that generic solutions can't touch.

Case Study

Automated Route Auditing for a Regional Utility Company

A regional utility company needed to visually audit thousands of service locations spread across multiple townships — an operation that would require enormous manual effort using traditional methods.

We designed a fully automated pipeline that started with the company's raw address data and turned it into optimized, flight-ready drone missions. Customer addresses were geocoded to GPS coordinates, then fed through a route optimization algorithm to minimize flight time and maximize coverage. The system generated terrain-aware mission files that accounted for local elevation changes, ensuring consistent above-ground altitude and safe operations.

After each flight, the captured imagery was automatically processed — photos matched back to specific customer addresses using GPS metadata, classified by angle, annotated with account details, and uploaded to cloud storage with time-limited shareable links. The final step: automated delivery of professional reports and photo packages directly to stakeholders via email.

The result was a system that compressed what would have been weeks of manual coordination, flying, sorting, and reporting into a streamlined, repeatable process — reducing labor costs, eliminating human error in photo-to-address matching, and delivering audit-ready documentation on a predictable schedule.

  • Custom mission planning & route optimization
  • Automated photo processing & classification
  • GPS-based data matching & annotation
  • Cloud delivery with shareable links & reports
  • Terrain-aware flight automation
